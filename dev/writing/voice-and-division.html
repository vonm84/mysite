Your seeds (verbatim — your words)

"I am a singer by training, and I think having a voice is beyond just a vibration — is how you assert yourself in the world, it's your uniqueness — and it is fundamentally relational."

"Having 'a voice in something' does mean you are impacting something, you are transforming something, you are encountering other voices."

"The band IS WHERE THE LEARNING HAPPENS... It's how you learn practical musicianship!"

"I just see the separation problem, and I am someone who works to tighten community bonds through play, sharing subjectivities, memories, personal stories, etc."

"What interests me are... the sounds and musical vocabularies that everyone has already acquired that are unique to them — like idiolects."

"The process IS very much the point." / "Playing a drum alongside someone IS different than listening back to drumming."
